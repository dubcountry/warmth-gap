The warmth gap

Across three continents, the warmest climates hold some of the coldest homes. Latitude does not decide who stays warm. Income, building stock and fuel do.

Method and caveats. Eurostat figures load live from the data pull (sample values shown until then). tado° indoor temperatures are the published per-country averages from the report appendix; they cover smart-thermostat homes, an affluent subset that is not representative of the population. The US, Canada and EU measures use different definitions and are not directly comparable; the Three countries view shows why.
